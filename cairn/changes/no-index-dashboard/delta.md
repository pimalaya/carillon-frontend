## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Not indexable
The dashboard is a private app and is not crawled or indexed: `public/robots.txt` disallows all crawlers (search and AI) and `index.html` carries a `noindex, nofollow` robots meta. The public, indexable surface is the marketing site, not this app; no SEO artefacts are added here.
