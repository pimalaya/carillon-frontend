# i18n

The interface is localised with react-i18next in English and French, detected from the browser and remembered across visits.

### Requirement: react-i18next, en + fr
Localisation SHALL use react-i18next with English and French locales under `src/i18n/` (config + `locales/en.json` / `locales/fr.json`).

### Requirement: Detection and persistence
The language SHALL be detected from the browser, remembered in `localStorage` under `carillon.lang`, and reflected on `<html lang>`. A header language switcher SHALL let the user change it.

### Requirement: Incremental extraction
New user-facing strings SHALL be added under the shared key structure. Strings still holding English literals (the deeper onboarding wizard stages and the billing/settings inner cards) SHALL be extracted into the same structure as they are next touched.
